    MY STORY

    My name is Zakir Faizal and I am an Industrial Engineering graduate from the University of Toronto and a freelance web designer and developer. As a people-person I naturally empathize with those around me, and I use this skill in combination with the knowledge that I have gained in Human Factors Engineering, User Experience Design, and User Interface design to design and develop websites for small businesses and individuals.

    My journey as a developer began in high school, when I learned Java programming and I further developed this skill in undergraduate university as I applied Java to a variety of projects and assignments. I also added SQL to skills inventory in this time. I then learned python programming during my Masters degree and enhanced my SQL knowledge. I currently work for Bombardier Aviation, where I use Python, SQL, and VBA to conduct data analysis and data visualizations. Prior to starting this job, I studied HTML, CSS, and JavaScript on my own as I sought to be a freelance front and back-end web developer.

    Outside of my work life, I spend a lot of my time with my family helping out with household chores and socializing with friends and family. I am very passionate about cricket and Formula 1 racing, spending my spare time watching matches and races. My other passions include cats, flight simulation, and playing musical instruments such as the guitar and piano.

    The web design and development industry is extremely fast paced and requires constant practice to keep up with industry standards. With this in mind, I know that while I have learned a lot, I will always have a lot to learn. Here's to continuous learning and self growth!

    MY WORKFLOW

    1

    Information Gathering

    Prior to designing and developing, a significant amount of my time is spent in understanding my client. This includes addressing the following

    1. What is my client's background?
    2. What do they hope to achieve with a website?
    3. Who is my client's target audience?

    2

    Design Phase

    Once I have a thorough understanding of my client's needs, I begin with the design phase. This includes hand drawn sketches, prototyping, user testing, and visual design.

    3

    Development Phase

    With my client's approval of the proposed design in phase 2, I will begin developing using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, WordPress, any other development platform that suits the project's needs, or as requested by my client.
